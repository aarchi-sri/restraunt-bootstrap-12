To experience a Culinary Journey of Authentic Flavours, find an Outlet .


Savour the authentic taste of delectable north-west frontier cuisine served in a platter with royal generosity.



LEGACY

From Chaamp Tajdar to Raan-E-Sikandari, we serve the best of North Indian delicacies for the foodie’s delight. Traditional Punjabi folk music playing in the background while you enjoy your delicious meal makes for a captivating experience. We keep the flavours of the land intact and our culinary delights are unmatchable. The uncompromising quality of food at Punjab Grill is our legacy and we aim to surpass our own standards every time we serve you.





STORY

Established in 2001 by two veterans of Food & Beverage industry: Mr. Amit Burman and Mr. Rohit Aggarwal of Lite Bite Foods Pvt. Ltd., Punjab Grill set up multiple branches in prominent cities of the country. Since then, we are known as one of the topmost elite dining restaurants in the hospitality industry. With the recent set up of our branches in Bangkok, Singapore along with Washington DC, Punjab Grill has expanded its radius globally.
